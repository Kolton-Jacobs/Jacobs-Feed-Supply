Our Story

Jacobs Feed & Supply is a fictional small business created for a CMPA 3301 final project. The goal is to demonstrate planning, documentation, and a clean two-page website prototype that could realistically represent a local feed store.

In a real community, a store like this exists to support local ranchers, farmers, and families by providing reliable products and clear guidance without overcomplicating the process.

Mission

Provide dependable feed and supplies with service that’s helpful, honest, and consistent.

Values

  • Practical advice over sales pressure
  • Consistency and quality in core inventory
  • Respect for customers’ time and budget
  • Community-first mindset

Project Notes

This prototype focuses on structure and clarity. If expanded, the next steps would include adding product categories, photos, a simple promotions section, and a contact form (front-end only).
